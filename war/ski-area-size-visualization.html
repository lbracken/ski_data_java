Ski Area Size Visualization

Alpine ski areas come in all sizes. And while you can't quantify the character of a mountain, the tool below helps to visualize a ski area's size. Check out how your home mountain compares, or how the big mountains stack up to each other.

The graph is a representation of a ski area's vertical drop and skiable area. It does not model the actual shape of any particular mountain. I've collected this data from each ski area's website and wikipedia; please let me know if you find any missing or inaccurate data.
